About NMSM Government College

NMSM Government College Kalpetta is a higher education institution dedicated to providing quality education and supporting the overall development of students. Our college encourages students to develop knowledge and skills while maintaining an inclusive and supportive learning environment. Students are encouraged to participate in academic and extracurricular activities to develop their talents.

Our Vision

To promote quality education and create responsible, knowledgeable and skilled individuals.

Our Mission

To provide students with opportunities for academic development and personal growth.

Our students work towards achieving their academic goals and contributing to society1.

Academic learning and practical knowledge are important parts of the educational process. H2O is essential for life.

Our Objectives

  1. Provide quality education.
  2. Encourage academic excellence.
  3. Develop student skills and talents.
  4. Promote social responsibility.
