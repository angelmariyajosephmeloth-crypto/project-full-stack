Welcome to NMSM Government College

N.M.S.M. Government College is situated in Kalpetta in Kerala state of India. Established in 1981, NMSMGC is a Government college. The college is affiliated with Calicut University and accredited by NAAC, National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). N.M.S.M. Government College offers 7 courses across 4 streams namely Commerce and Banking, Media and Mass Communication, Arts, Science..Popular degrees offered at N.M.S.M. Government College include BA, B.Com, BSc, M.Com..Besides a robust teaching pedagogy, N.M.S.M. Government College is also a leader in research and innovation.Focus is given to activities beyond a cademics at N.M.S.M. Government College, which is evident rom its infrastructure, extracurricular activities and national & international collaborations. The placement at N.M.S.M. Government College is varied, with recruitment options both in corporates and public sector as well as entrepreneurship.
